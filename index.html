Who We Are

PlayChange Foundation is organized by students from the Department of Physical Education and Sport Studies in the University of Ghana that uses sport as a tool for social development. We believe that sport has the power to transform lives and communities.

Our Mission

To inspire development and change through sports and play.

Our Vision

A Ghana where everyone can achieve their full potential through sport and physical activity.

Our Values

Inclusiveness, respect, innovation, and collaboration.

SDGs in Focus

Our work aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, contributing to a better and more sustainable future for all through sports and community development.

SDG 3: Good Health

Good Health and Well-being

Promoting physical activity and healthy lifestyles through sports programs that improve community health and well-being.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Quality Education

Using sports as an educational tool to teach life skills, teamwork, and leadership to youth across Ghana.

SDG 5: Gender Equality

Gender Equality

Creating inclusive sports programs that empower girls and women, promoting equal opportunities for all.

SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities

Reduced Inequalities

Ensuring sports access for all communities regardless of socioeconomic status, creating equal opportunities.

SDG 11: Sustainable Communities

Sustainable Cities and Communities

Building resilient communities through sports infrastructure and programs that foster social cohesion.

SDG 17: Partnerships

Partnerships for the Goals

Collaborating with local and international partners to amplify our impact and achieve sustainable development.

How We Align Safeguarding to Our Work

At PlayChange Foundation, safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to creating safe, inclusive, and protective environments for all participants, especially children and vulnerable individuals.

Child Protection

Comprehensive policies and procedures to protect children from harm, abuse, and exploitation in all our programs.

Safe Recruitment

Rigorous background checks and vetting processes for all staff, volunteers, and partners working with our communities.

Training & Awareness

Regular safeguarding training for all team members to recognize, respond to, and report concerns appropriately.

Code of Conduct

Clear behavioral standards and ethical guidelines that all participants, staff, and partners must adhere to.

Reporting Mechanisms

Accessible and confidential channels for reporting safeguarding concerns, with prompt investigation and action.

Risk Assessment

Continuous evaluation of potential risks in our programs and implementation of preventive measures.

Inclusive Environment

Creating respectful spaces where diversity is celebrated and everyone feels safe, valued, and protected.

Accountability

Regular monitoring, evaluation, and transparent reporting on our safeguarding practices and outcomes.

Our Commitment

We believe that safeguarding is everyone's responsibility. By embedding these principles into our organizational culture, we ensure that sports remain a positive force for development while protecting the dignity, rights, and well-being of all participants.
